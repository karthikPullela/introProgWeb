FAQ


Here are some frequently asked questions:



  • Are there any fees?
  • Yes; the club fee is $100.

  • Who is eligible?
  • The club is suggested for 7th and 8th grades. However, upper school studens may also join if they are interested in learning web programming.

  • Is previous programming knowledge required?
  • No prior knowledge of programming is required.

  • What do you plan on covering by the end of this year?
  • We plan to cover as much as possible of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, in that order.

  • Do the students need to bring their own laptops?
  • That is not necessary. The school provides with laptops.

  • Will there be any homework?
  • No, there is no homework. The class will be taught in a no pressure environment to make it more interesting an fun without compromising any learning. The students are required to bring a notebook to class to take down notes to help them refer to what is taught in class.
